TikTok Integration — UI

Use this interface to authenticate with TikTok (sandbox or production) and send video metadata to your automation webhook.

Authentication

Click to open TikTok Login. After authorization the redirect will return to your configured callback (n8n or app).

Redirect URI (must match your app): http://localhost:5678/rest/oauth2-credential/callback

Upload Video Metadata

Choose a file (optional) then provide caption, hashtags and visibility. Click Send to n8n Webhook to POST this data to your workflow.
